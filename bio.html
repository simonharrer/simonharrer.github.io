Bio (English)

Dr. Simon Harrer is the Co-Founder and CEO of Entropy Data, a data marketplace built on data products and data contracts. He is the author of the best-selling book "Java by Comparison", co-author of "Remote Mob Programming" and "GitOps", and translator of the German edition of "Data Mesh". He is the creator of the popular open source tools Data Contract CLI, Data Contract Editor, and mob.sh. Before founding Entropy Data, he worked as a Senior Consultant at INNOQ and earned his Dr. in Computer Science from Otto-Friedrich-Universität Bamberg.
